Welcome to Scranton, PA

Here in the electic city, we like to play games. Michael Scott, our regional manager, created this Hangman-Style Game. Unfortunately, our assistant TO the regional manager, Dwight Shrute, is not pleased with playing games on company time.

The game is simple:

  • Guess a letter that involves our office
  • If you guess the secret word, Michael will reward you!
  • If you run out of turns, fear Dwight!
